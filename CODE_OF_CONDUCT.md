# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

TouchAI is an open source project. We expect discussions and reviews to stay respectful, direct, and technically grounded.

Examples of expected behavior:

- discuss ideas and code in good faith
- assume mistakes before malice
- give specific, reproducible feedback
- respect different levels of experience and different backgrounds
- keep criticism focused on the work, not the person

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, threats, intimidation, or stalking
- personal attacks, insults, or bad-faith escalation
- discriminatory or exclusionary language
- publishing private information without permission
- repeated disruption of discussions, reviews, or moderation decisions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, lock, or close content that violates this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent removal from the project spaces.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer through the contact method listed on the repository or organization profile. If you cannot find a private route, open a GitHub Discussion without sensitive details and request a private follow-up.

Security-sensitive reports should follow `SECURITY.md`, not public issues.
